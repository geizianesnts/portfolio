Improving the UI/UX of 
INTELLIbed: A Case Study

INTELLIbed  is an innovative company that develops smart bed bases which can be controlled by a mobile app.

In this project, I researched INTELLIbed's app user experience and gave suggestions on how to enhance the app for users. The project’s goal was to redesign the app to be more appealing by adding personality, and improve the overall flow.

A wireframe was developed with ideas for a more user-friendly interface following the Information Architecture (IA) principles, a color scheme was created focusing on the app’s purpose, and a prototype was used on Figma to test the new design.  

Choosing The Color Scheme

Colors are more than just visual elements; they create mood, support functionality, and convey brand identity. INTELLIbed’s Mobile app was designed focusing on bedtime usage, which aims to promote relaxation and restful sleep, the color scheme should prioritize calming and soothing tones to help users unwind.



Prototyping with  Figma

This process helps save time and resources by allowing changes to be made at an early stage, ensuring the final product is both user-friendly and aligned with business goals. Prototyping bridges the gap between ideas and implementation, making it a crucial step in creating effective, intuitive mobile applications.
